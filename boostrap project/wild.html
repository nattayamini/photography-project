Pinocchio and Beauty and the Beast. The brilliant animations crafted after many hours observing movements and characteristics of the creatures depicted, coupled with rich characterizations, witty and entertaining lyrics, memorable musical arrangements and creative, humorous dialogue ensures the film has wide appeal to primary aged students, older students and to adults.The central theme of The Lion King, 'The Circle of Life' conveys the ideas that all individuals have a place in the world and that all living things are interdependent, relying on each other for survival. 'The Circle of Life' is conveyed visually at the beginning of the film with the presentation of the future lion king, and concludes with his ascendancy to the throne and birth of his son--again, the future lion king. With these images we see life coming full circle.A number of virtues are explored within this central theme.
